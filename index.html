Welcome to TayTay Crochet

I'm Tay, and this is my corner of the internet where yarn, ideas, and slow projects live together. Think of this as a cozy journal, not a polished storefront.

Letter from the Author

Crochet has always been the place my brain goes when the rest of life feels too loud. I like slow stitches, long projects, and the kind of making that doesn’t care about deadlines. This site is where I keep track of what I’m working on, what I’m thinking about, and what I want to try next.

If you’re here, you’re invited to treat this like a quiet room: read a bit, wander through the works-in-progress, and maybe leave with an idea you want to try with your own hands.
