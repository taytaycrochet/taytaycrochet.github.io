Everything Autumn Crochet

When the weather starts to get cold, a good pair of mittens, a beanie, or a cardigan are incredibly useful! What tops it off is being able to make them yourself. Your family will flock to you to make them similar pieces and - only if you have time and extra yarn - really it's just good practice (or content) for you. To those who truly love crafting, I am with you in committing the time to MAKE my fall pieces instead of just buying for convenience sake. Good luck to all of you! Please share your creations with me, I would love to see them!
